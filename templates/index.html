Input a Team Name

The index '0' corresponds to the 2019 stats, whereas the '1' index refers to stats from 2020.
My custom stat is the 'Missed stops' statistic - which weights each stat accordingly to give an indication
of how often a defense can 'get off the field'.Teams with low Missed Stop % tend to have strong defenses.

Input a QB Full Name

The index '0' corresponds to the 2019 stats, whereas the '1' index refers to stats from 2020.
My custom stat is the 'Efficient Yards' statistic - which weights each stat accordingly to give an indication
of how efficient a QB is- an adjustment over the Yards per Att metric. QBs with high Effiecent Yards over Average tend to be the Star QBs
