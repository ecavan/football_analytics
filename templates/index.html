Input a NFL Team Name

The index '0' corresponds to the 2019 stats, whereas the '1' index refers to stats from 2020.
My custom stat is the 'Missed stops' statistic - which weights each stat accordingly to give an indication
of how often a defense can 'get off the field'.Teams with low Missed Stop % tend to have strong defenses.

Input a NFL QB Full Name

The index '0' corresponds to the 2019 stats, whereas the '1' index refers to stats from 2020.
My custom stat is the 'Efficient Yards' statistic - which weights each stat accordingly to give an indication
of how efficient a QB is- an adjustment over the Yards per Att metric. QBs with high Effiecent Yards over Average tend to be the Star QBs

Input a Blue Jays Hitter Last Name

Input a last name to see 2020 stats for some of the top hitters on this years Toronto Blue Jays Team.
Options: Bichette, Guerrero Jr., Hernandez, Semien, Springer, Biggio, Grichuk (remember the period after Jr for Vladdy)

Input a Points Scored Number

A simple model based on the observation that as you score more in basketball, the defence has to focus on you more opening up oppertunities for your teamates.
It's also clear that these points are harder to come by because of that defence. Put in a number and you will recieve an adjusted point total (ex. 33 points is worth 35.55 points in this model).
